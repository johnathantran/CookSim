CookSim


CookSim is a cooking simulator that teaches you how to cook in real life. How exactly does that work? Itâ€™s actually pretty simple. In CookSim, you work as a professional chef in Central City and cook for many different restaurants. Your job is to serve virtual customers by making the requested dish in real life and uploading a photo of your cooking to our site. Over time, you will learn to cook different kinds of cuisine by fulfilling orders for restaurants all around Central City. Our system is intuitive and rewarding. We introduce you to culturally diverse foods, keep track of your personal culinary milestones, and reward you for your achievements as a growing cook. You receive credits for completing orders that you can use to be promoted. Of course, more advanced positions at your restaurants will require you to cook more complex dishes. So what are you waiting for? Get cooking with CookSim.

Getting Started


Select a restaurant in the city by clicking on the city map. Once you've selected a location, pick one of the dishes to cook, snap a photo, and upload the image. Once your image has been processed, we'll serve it to the customer and you give you credits. You can use your credits to unlock job promotions so you can learn to cook more complex meals.
